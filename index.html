Who am I?

I’m Susant Shrestha, Frontend Developer based in Kathmandu,Nepal that’s passionate about creating intuitive design solutions that enhance users’ experience while meeting their needs in an inspiring way.

My work is driven by a single goal: crafting delightful experiences through a combination of user-centred research, streamlined workflow processes and open-minded iterative collaboration.

Beyond design, my range of interests include sci-fi and fantasy movies & TV shows, calligraphy, and continuous learning.

Around two years of experience in planning, requirement analysis, design, and developing a web application. Experience with design patterns, Object Oriented Systems Design. Able to work in both team and self-directed environment with quick learning and mastering new technology.
